About

It is a fact that we are too preoccupied with work, school, and other tasks. Our body is aging, and as we age, it gets a lot of problem/health issues. It is by general and common knowledge that being active physically( working out, stretching, etc.) can lessen or even avoid such health risks. The pandemic is still prevailing, all of us are doing lesser pyhsical activivities because of the protocols, such as not being able to go out (cant walk more often like before), gyms are closed and more, that prevents us to become physically active. With the help of this website, maybe it can change lives, because it will be guiding anyone who are dedicated to adding WORKOUT as their daily or weekly activity, one step at a time.
